Treatments

Considered, private dentistry for every stage of life

From routine family care to cosmetic work and implants, every treatment is planned around you by the same small team that knows your history.

General & Family

Examinations, hygiene, fillings, crowns, bridges, dentures, root canal and extractions. The everyday care that keeps a family healthy.

Clear Aligners

Discreet teeth-straightening with removable aligner trays, scanned and planned in-practice for a comfortable, low-visibility fit.

Dental Implants

Single, multiple and full-arch implant solutions to replace missing teeth, restoring function and confidence with a fixed result.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Composite bonding, veneers, inlays and onlays, Air Flow polishing and teeth whitening, planned for a natural, balanced look.

Teeth Whitening

Professionally supervised whitening to gently brighten your natural shade, with take-home trays fitted to your teeth.

Emergency Care

Pain, breaks and lost fillings handled quickly by a team that already knows you, so urgent problems are seen with priority.

Common questions

What patients ask us most

A few of the things people most often want to know before they get in touch with the practice.

Is Gentle Dental Care private or NHS?
The practice is fully private and independently owned. It is not part of any NHS contract or corporate dental group, so your care is planned around you rather than around a quota.
Are you accepting new patients?
To be honest with you, the practice is currently full and is running a waiting list for new patients. You are welcome to register your interest using the form below, and the team will be in touch when a space opens up. Joining the list does not book an appointment.
What is the membership plan?
The practice offers a range of membership plans that let you spread the cost of routine care across the year as one predictable monthly payment. Plans typically include your routine examinations and hygiene appointments. Ask the team and they will talk you through which plan suits you.
Do you offer Invisalign, cosmetic dentistry or implants?
Yes. Alongside general and family dentistry, the practice provides clear aligner teeth-straightening, cosmetic treatments such as composite bonding, veneers and whitening, and single, multiple or full-arch dental implants. Your dentist will discuss the options that are suitable for you before any treatment begins.
Where are you located?
You will find the practice at 85 South Street, St Andrews, Fife, KY16 9QW, in the heart of the town. There is a map further down this page to help you find your way.
What does "independent" mean for my care?
It means the practice answers to its patients, not to a head office or shareholders. There are no corporate sales targets, you tend to see the same familiar team who know your history, and treatment is recommended because it is right for you. The practice is privately owned and led by Dr Chris Pritchard.
How do I get in touch?
You can call the practice on 01334 475 433, email info@standrewsdentist.co.uk, or fill in the waiting-list form below. The team is happy to answer any questions you have about treatments or membership.
